Algo'Rythmic is the annual Tech-festival organized by the students of Govt. Engineering College, Bikaner. Etymologically, the word 'AlgoRythmic' is composed of two words; 'Algo' is short term for Algorithm "set of instructions for solving a problem or completing a process" and 'Rythmic' stands for having rhythm. Hence, the word 'AlgoRythmic' signifies solving problem with rhythm.
