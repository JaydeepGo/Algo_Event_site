Treasure Hunt

A treasure hunt is one of many different types of games with one or more players who try to find hidden objects or places by following a series of clues. Treasure hunt games may be an indoor or outdoor activity. Outdoors they can be played in a garden or the treasure could be located anywhere around the world.

Screvanger Hunt

A scavenger hunt is a party game in which the organizers prepare a list defining specific items, which the participants seek to gather or complete all items on the list, usually without purchasing them. Usually participants work in small teams, although the rules may allow individuals to participate. The goal is to be the first to complete the list or to complete the most items on the list.

Paper Dance

Partners dance on their own newspaper. Each time the music stops, the paper is cut or folded in half. The partner must continue to dance on the newspaper without touching the floor. The last partner to stay dancing on their newspaper is the winner.
